About Me

I am working as a Data Scientist at Project 990 Inc., where I build ETL workflows across multiple sources and organize grant records so teams can track funding patterns by year and region. Earlier, I was a Machine Learning Intern at ManekTech, where I built a backend for real-time AQI forecasting, and a Data Science & Business Analytics Intern at Start Tech Academy, where I worked on image and text classification.

I have also worked on projects such as a RAG-based AI Knowledge Assistant, Recommender Systems, AR Video playback on print media and Social Network Platform. Alongside project work, I regularly write technical blogs focused on data engineering, RAG pipelines, LLM frameworks, AI systems, and modern ML infrastructure. I hold a Master’s degree in Data Science from Indiana University and a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science from Indus University.
